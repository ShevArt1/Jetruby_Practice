EXISTING COMMANDS

/deposit?value=a - deposit 100

/deposit?value=b - deposit 500

/deposit?value=c - deposit 1000

/deposit?value=10 - deposit 10 (can use any other number)

/withdraw?value=10 - withdraw 10 (can use any other number)

/balance - see your current balance

/quit - save your current balance and quit
